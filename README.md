## Project Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive Sales Performance Analysis for Vrinda Store for the year 2022. The project involves data cleaning, processing, and the creation of an interactive dashboard in Excel to uncover key business insights regarding customer demographics, regional sales, and distribution channels.

## Project Objective
To analyze store data and provide actionable insights that help the business understand its primary customer base, peak sales periods, and the most effective platforms for growth in 2023.

## Key Insights & Findings
Target Audience: Women are the primary customers, contributing to 69% of total sales.

Top Demographics: The "Adult" age group is the highest-contributing segment.

Geographic Performance: Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, and Karnataka are the top three performing states.

Channel Efficiency: Amazon is the leading sales channel (35%), followed by Myntra (23%) and Flipkart (22%).

Order Health: The store maintains a high fulfillment rate, with 92% of orders successfully delivered.

## Features of the Dashboard
The interactive dashboard (shown in the Vrinda Store Data Analysis.xlsx file) includes:

Dynamic Slicers: Filter data by Month, Product Category (Kurta, Saree, Western, etc.), and Sales Channel.

Orders vs. Sales Trend: A monthly breakdown comparing the number of orders against total revenue.

Customer Segmentation: Visual breakdown of sales by Gender and Age Group.

Geographical Mapping: A bar chart highlighting the Top 5 performing states.

Order Status Tracking: A donut chart showing the ratio of Delivered, Cancelled, and Returned orders.

## Technical Stack
Tool: Microsoft Excel

Techniques: Data Cleaning, Pivot Tables, Data Visualization, Interactive Slicers, and Dashboard Design.

## How to Use
Download the Vrinda Store Data Analysis.xlsx file.

Open it in Microsoft Excel.

Use the Slicers at the top of the "Dashboard" sheet to interact with the data and see specific monthly or category-wise trends.
